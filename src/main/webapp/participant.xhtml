Beforehand, I would like to thank you for your time and for being part of this experiment.

The following are the steps to succesfuly carry out the experiment:
1. Click on the Start button to start the experiment.
2. You will see a list with all the tags placed on your ZEEF pages, please select one. This will be your query tag.
3. Once you've selected your query tag you will automatically be redirected to the next step.
4. The system will show ~30 recommended tags, please evaluate each of them in terms of its relevancy to the query tag.
5. Repeat steps 2-4 five times selecting a different query tag each round.
